# Orbs Knowledge Base

This folder is a dedicated knowledge base for the Orbs project.

## Purpose
- Support fact lookup about Orbs
- Explain historical, technical, business, and partnership context
- Accumulate reusable material for Q&A, briefings, and research

## Operating Principles
- Prioritize official sources
- Distinguish facts, interpretations, and unverified points
- Store long-form source material under `07_sources/` and curated knowledge under the category folders
- Record notable changes in `08_notes/update-log.md`
- Treat exchange information as live references rather than fixed static lists
- If a detail is not covered in this knowledge base, do not fill the gap with ad hoc external searching, route the user to official contact or community channels instead
- Keep English as the canonical language of the knowledge base
- Handle Korean and other non-English content as a separate summary or briefing layer
- Preserve English source terms for product names, role names, and technical concepts

## Language Policy
- Base documents under `00_overview` through `09_training` should remain in English
- If Korean material is needed, prefer summary notes, briefs, and audience-specific Q&A over full duplicate translations
- Even in Korean delivery artifacts, keep key terms such as `Delegator`, `Guardian`, and `Liquidity Hub` visible in English
- If terminology translation needs to become strict and repeatable, define it later in a separate glossary or translation standard

## Response Policy
- For exchange listing questions, direct users to the ORBS market pages on `CoinMarketCap` and `CoinGecko`
- For details not covered in the knowledge base, direct users to official community channels and `hello@orbs.com`
- If country-specific community information is needed, route through the official community entry points

## Suggested Workflow
1. Add raw source links to `07_sources/raw-links.md`
2. Organize official sources under `07_sources/official/`
3. Build or refine `00_overview/key-facts.md`
4. Build or refine `00_overview/timeline.md`
5. Fill in founders, history, technology, use-cases, and business documents
6. Refine `09_training/glossary.md` and `09_training/entity-map.md`
7. Build or refine `06_qna/faq.md` and related anticipated-question files
