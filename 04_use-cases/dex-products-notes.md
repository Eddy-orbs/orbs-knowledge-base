# DEX Product Notes

## dTWAP
- Publicly described as decentralized time-weighted average price orders.
- Core purpose: split orders into smaller chunks to reduce price impact.

## dLIMIT
- Publicly described as decentralized limit orders with on-chain execution guarantees.
- Public materials describe it as based on dTWAP.
- Production note from public snippet: dTWAP had reportedly been live since early 2023 and processed millions in trading volume across multiple chains and DEXs.

## dSLTP
- Publicly described as decentralized stop-loss and take-profit orders with on-chain execution guarantees.

## Liquidity Hub
- Presented as a DEX liquidity standard / liquidity optimization layer.

## Perpetual Hub
- Described as a suite for intent-based perpetual futures, powered by Orbs L3 execution services.

## Orbs Agentic
- Described as an execution layer for autonomous DeFi agents.

## Execution Services
- Orbs VM and Orbs Lambda are described as decentralized execution services, often compared conceptually to EC2 and Lambda respectively, but decentralized.
