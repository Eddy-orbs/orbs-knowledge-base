# Investor Brief (EN)

Orbs should currently be viewed less as a generic Layer-1 and more as a DeFi execution infrastructure stack. Its differentiation comes from productized execution services such as advanced orders, liquidity optimization, perpetuals infrastructure, and agent execution. The ORBS token connects network incentives through staking, Guardian selection, fee settlement, and rewards. In practical market terms, the most visible adoption surface appears to be protocol integrations, especially around dLIMIT and dTWAP-style products.
