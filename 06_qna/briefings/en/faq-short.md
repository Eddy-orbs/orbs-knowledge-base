# Short FAQ (EN)

## What is Orbs?
Orbs is a decentralized Layer-3 execution layer for DeFi.

## Is Orbs a Layer-1?
Not in its current primary positioning. It is better described as a supplementary execution layer for DeFi protocols.

## What is ORBS used for?
Fee settlement, staking, Guardian selection, and rewards.

## What if a requested detail is missing?
Do not guess or rely on ad hoc external searching. Route the user to official contact or community channels such as `hello@orbs.com`.
