# Founders Overview

## Summary
Based on the official-public materials collected so far together with additional user-provided notes, the Orbs co-founders can be summarized as Daniel Peled, Netta Korin, Tal Kol, and Uriel Peled. In May 2020, Nadav Shemesh became CEO, marking an important operating-structure transition.

## Founders
- Daniel Peled
  - Publicly referenced as President and Co-founder
  - Role area: business, strategy, and external representation
- Netta Korin
  - Publicly referenced as Co-founder and head of the Hexa Foundation
  - Role area: ecosystem, foundation, public affairs, and social impact
- Tal Kol
  - Publicly referenced as a technological co-founder or CTO-like figure
  - Role area: technology and engineering leadership
- Uriel Peled
  - Publicly referenced as Co-founder
  - Role area: early business, product, ecosystem, and partnerships

## CEO Transition
- Nadav Shemesh
  - Became CEO in May 2020
  - Not a founder, but important to the project’s operating history

## Relationship Map
- Daniel Peled -> Co-founder / President -> Orbs
- Netta Korin -> Co-founder / Hexa Foundation -> Orbs ecosystem
- Tal Kol -> Co-founder / technical leadership -> Orbs technology
- Uriel Peled -> Co-founder / early business-product role -> Orbs
- Nadav Shemesh -> CEO from 2020-05 -> Orbs operations

## Working Interpretation
- Daniel Peled and Netta Korin appear frequently in outward-facing business and ecosystem contexts
- Tal Kol is the clearest technology founder in the currently collected material
- Uriel Peled appears to have played a meaningful early founder role and later stepped back from day-to-day management
- Nadav Shemesh marks an important managerial phase shift beginning in 2020
