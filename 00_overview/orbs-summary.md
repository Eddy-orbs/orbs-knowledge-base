# Orbs Summary

## One-line Summary
Orbs is a blockchain infrastructure project currently positioned as a decentralized Layer-3 and execution layer focused on bringing CeFi-level execution capabilities to DeFi.

- What kind of project is Orbs?

## Key Facts
- Official messaging emphasizes advanced on-chain trading and DeFi execution.
- Orbs uses a Proof-of-Stake model.
- Public materials connect Orbs staking and token infrastructure to Ethereum, with later expansion toward Polygon-based staking.
- Product and use-case pages highlight dLIMIT, dTWAP, Liquidity Hub, Perpetual Hub, Agentic, and broader execution services.

## Short Overview
At the current stage, Orbs appears to be positioned less as a general-purpose Layer-1 and more as an execution infrastructure layer attached to DeFi protocols and DEXs. The core official message is "CeFi-level execution to DeFi," with emphasis on advanced order types, liquidity aggregation, derivatives support, and execution services.

Technically, Orbs is built around a PoS-based network model. Public materials link ORBS staking and token-related contracts to Ethereum, with later expansion toward Polygon-based staking. The relationship between the project’s earlier positioning and its current positioning still deserves further document-level verification.
