# People Overview

## Purpose
This section tracks notable Orbs-related people who are important to user-facing explanations but should not be mixed into the founder list.

## Categories
### Founders
The Orbs co-founders are tracked separately under `01_founders/`.

### CEO
CEO-transition information, especially Nadav Shemesh becoming CEO in May 2020, is tracked separately from both the founder list and general executive profiles.

### Key Executives / General Team Members
This category is for notable Orbs Ltd. executives and team members who are neither founders nor CEO, but are important for understanding Orbs' current organization, business, ecosystem, or operations.

Current entries:
- Ran Hammer (란 해머) — Orbs Ltd. CBO / business lead

## Answering Rule
When asked about a person, first classify the person correctly:
- Founder
- CEO
- Key executive / general team member
- External partner / ecosystem participant
- Unknown / insufficiently supported

Do not place key executives such as Ran Hammer in the founder list or CEO role.
