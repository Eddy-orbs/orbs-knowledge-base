## April 2026 Governance / Ecosystem Updates
- Orbs entered the next phase of its DAO rollout in April 2026. The DAO is intended to expand governance from network operations into protocol-level strategy and capital allocation.

## May 2026 SPOT / Agent-Native DeFi Update
- On 2026-05-01, Orbs publicly announced SPOT / Spot Advanced Swap Orders as a DeFi trading interface built natively for AI agents.
- SPOT is best understood as an agent-readable and wallet-signable order interface layered over Orbs' existing DeFi execution primitives, rather than a custodial bot.
- Supported order shapes include gasless market swaps, limit orders, TWAP, stop-loss, take-profit, delayed-start swaps, and recurring/chunked execution.
- This strengthens the current-state narrative: Orbs is not only a DeFi execution layer for DEX UX, but also a potential execution layer for autonomous or semi-autonomous AI-agent DeFi workflows.
- The main risk framing is no longer just execution quality; agent permissions, approval scope, strategy constraints, and policy guardrails become important user-facing topics.

## May 2026 Orbs Agentic Challenge
- The Orbs Agentic Challenge runs from 2026-05-04 to 2026-05-24, UTC, as a two-phase Orbs Agentic trading campaign.
- Phase 1 is an onboarding raffle: one Orbs Agentic trade during 2026-05-04 to 2026-05-10 enters the participant into a 500 USDC raffle with 10 random winners.
- Phase 2 runs 2026-05-11 to 2026-05-24 and combines trading-volume prizes with a lucky draw: approximately 599 USDC for first by volume, 200 USDC for second, 100 USDC for third, plus 10 random 50 USDC prizes.
- The campaign is useful current-state evidence for Orbs' agentic DeFi go-to-market narrative: it asks users to install / use the Orbs Agentic skill and execute order types such as TWAP, limit order, or stop-loss through an agent-native flow.
