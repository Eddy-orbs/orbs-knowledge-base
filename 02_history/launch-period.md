# Launch Period

## Summary
The Orbs launch period can be defined around the March 2019 mainnet and token launch. Official materials and blog snippets repeatedly refer to a March 2019 mainnet launch, and PoS V1 is tied to this same phase.

## Launch Events
- 2019-03: Orbs mainnet launch
- 2019-03: ORBS token launch and distribution-related events
- 2019-03: PoS V1 introduction

## Technical / Market Significance
- Public materials describe PoS V1 as launching alongside the Orbs mainnet and emphasize a hybrid structure that used Ethereum as an external security or oversight layer
- Because PoS V2, V2.5, and V3 came later, the launch-era architecture serves as the baseline for understanding subsequent evolution
- From a market and business perspective, this period is frequently treated as the starting point for later ecosystem growth and project repositioning

## Important Notes
- Some materials imply that mainnet launch and token-distribution events occurred in late March 2019
- Exact day-level timing and the relationship between mainnet launch, token launch, and distribution events should still be checked against the original blog and whitepaper sources
