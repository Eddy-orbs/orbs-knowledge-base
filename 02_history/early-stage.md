# Early Stage

## Summary
The early stage of Orbs covers the period from the project’s formation in 2017 up to the mainnet launch. Based on the materials gathered so far, Orbs built its early identity around PoS and hybrid blockchain architecture before moving into the mainnet era in March 2019.

## Key Events
- 2017: project formation begins
- 2018 to early 2019: mainnet preparation, community and enterprise-facing introduction efforts, and early PoS architecture design
- Pre-2019-03: final technical and ecosystem preparation before mainnet launch

## Strategic Context
- In its early phase, Orbs appears to have emphasized blockchain infrastructure and PoS design more than the current DeFi execution-layer narrative
- Hybrid structure, the use of an external chain such as Ethereum for security and oversight, and broader PoS ecosystem design all appear to have been important themes
- Founder narrative and technical narrative from this period still need stronger recovery from official materials

## What Needs More Evidence
- Founder-by-founder role allocation and early contributions
- A more exact year-by-year sequence of events across 2017 and 2018
- Early market-positioning language
- Early partner, investment, and regional-expansion history
