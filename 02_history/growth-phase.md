# Growth Phase

## Summary
Orbs의 성장 단계는 메인넷 런칭 이후 PoS 구조의 고도화와 제품 포지셔닝의 전환으로 요약할 수 있습니다. 초기 네트워크 안정화 이후, Orbs는 V2/V2.5/V3를 거치며 staking 구조를 진화시켰고, 최근에는 DeFi execution layer로서의 제품군 확장에 집중하고 있습니다.

## Growth Drivers
- Mainnet launch completed in 2019
- PoS architecture evolution (V1 -> V2 -> V2.5 -> V3)
- Expansion of product suite for DeFi execution
- Increasing integrations with DeFi protocols and DEXs

## Partnerships / Ecosystem Expansion
- Staking ecosystem visibility through platforms such as Staking Rewards
- Historical ecosystem tie-ins such as Celsius
- Product-led integrations through dLIMIT, dTWAP, Liquidity Hub, and Perpetual Hub

## Strategic Shift
- Early Orbs narrative appears more protocol / architecture centered
- Current growth narrative is much more product and adoption centered, especially around advanced trading infrastructure for DeFi
