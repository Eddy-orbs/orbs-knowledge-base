# Growth Phase

## Summary
The Orbs growth phase can be summarized as a combination of post-mainnet PoS evolution and product-positioning change. After the initial network-stabilization period, Orbs evolved its staking structure through V2, V2.5, and V3, while more recently focusing on expanding its DeFi execution-layer product suite.

## Growth Drivers
- Mainnet launch completed in 2019
- PoS architecture evolution from V1 to V2 to V2.5 to V3
- Expansion of the product suite for DeFi execution
- Increasing integrations with DeFi protocols and DEXs

## Partnerships / Ecosystem Expansion
- Staking ecosystem visibility through platforms such as Staking Rewards
- Historical ecosystem tie-ins such as Celsius
- Product-led integrations through dLIMIT, dTWAP, Liquidity Hub, and Perpetual Hub

## Strategic Shift
- The early Orbs narrative appears more protocol- and architecture-centered
- The current growth narrative is much more product- and adoption-centered, especially around advanced trading infrastructure for DeFi
