# Token Distribution Notes

## Summary
The ORBS token distribution structure is described in a dedicated token-distribution document covering total supply and the relative size of allocation pools. Based on the public information gathered so far, there is official reference material describing team and founding-partner allocation at the 2019-03-28 TDE.

## Confirmed / Publicly Referenced Points
- Token distribution has a dedicated official paper
- The document explains token use, total supply, and allocation pools
- Public official references indicate that at the 2019-03-28 TDE, 20% of total supply was allocated to the team and founding partners
- Public official references also indicate that this team and founding-partner pool had a 36-month vesting schedule and an initial 6-month lock-up period
- Later official communications note that team tokens were voluntarily placed under the same locking mechanism used in the PoS universe and did not participate in PoS elections or share staking rewards

## Why This Matters
- It directly affects interpretation of founder, treasury, ecosystem, and rewards narratives
- Distribution history matters when explaining token economics and staking incentives
- It also matters for long-term vesting, unlocking, and market-overhang narratives

## Documentation Guidance
- Distribution structure should be documented from the official distribution paper first
- Blog-post snippets should be used only as supporting references
- The final allocation table should be reconstructed directly from source documents later
