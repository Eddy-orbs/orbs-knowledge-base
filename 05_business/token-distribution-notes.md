# Token Distribution Notes

## Summary
ORBS 토큰 분배 구조는 별도 token distribution 문서로 설명되며, total supply와 각 allocation pool의 비중을 다룹니다. 현재까지 확보한 공개 정보 기준으로, 2019-03-28 TDE 시점에 팀 및 founding partners allocation 관련 공식 언급이 존재합니다.

## Confirmed / Publicly Referenced Points
- Token distribution has a dedicated official paper.
- The document explains token use, total supply, and distribution pools.
- Public official reference indicates that at the TDE on 2019-03-28, 20% of total supply was allocated to the team and founding partners.
- Public official reference also indicates that this team/founding-partner pool had a 36-month vesting schedule and an initial 6-month lock-up period.
- Later official communications note that team tokens were voluntarily placed under the same locking mechanism used in the PoS universe and did not participate in PoS elections or share staking rewards.

## Why This Matters
- founder / treasury / ecosystem / rewards interpretation에 직접 연결됨
- token economics와 staking incentives를 설명할 때 distribution history가 중요함
- 장기 vesting / unlocking / market overhang narrative와도 연결될 수 있음

## Documentation Guidance
- 분배 구조는 반드시 공식 distribution paper 기준으로 정리
- blog post snippets는 보조적으로만 사용
- allocation table은 추후 직접 문서 기반으로 재구성
