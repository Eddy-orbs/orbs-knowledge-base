# Bridge References

## Summary
ORBS token bridging is an operationally important topic because the token exists across multiple chain environments and some staking / ecosystem participation flows may depend on moving assets to the relevant network.

## Official / Publicly Referenced Bridges
- Polygon official bridge
- Axelar / Satellite bridge references for multi-chain expansion
- BNB bridge reference
- Arbitrum bridge reference
- Historical Multichain / AnySwap references in older posts
- Wormhole reference for Solana expansion

## Practical Relevance
- Users may need bridges to move ORBS for ecosystem participation on a specific chain
- Bridge availability can affect liquidity distribution and market access
- Documentation should distinguish official current bridges from historical bridge announcements

## Caution
- Bridge providers change over time.
- Older blog posts may mention historical bridge partners that are no longer the recommended path.
- Final user guidance should prioritize currently active and official routes.
