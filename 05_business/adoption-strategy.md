# Adoption Strategy

## Summary
Based on current public materials, Orbs’ adoption strategy appears closer to integrating execution products into DEXs and DeFi protocols than to acquiring end users directly through standalone consumer applications.

## Strategy Components
- Integrate advanced order products into DEXs
- Become the execution layer behind improved DeFi trading UX
- Expand from spot-trading enhancements into liquidity and perpetuals infrastructure
- Extend into autonomous agent execution

## Working Interpretation
Rather than trying to own all liquidity or trading activity directly, Orbs appears to follow a B2B2C-style infrastructure strategy that upgrades the trading experience of other protocols.
