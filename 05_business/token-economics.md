# Token Economics

## Summary
공개 자료 기준 ORBS 토큰은 네트워크 수수료 정산, staking, Guardians 선출, PoS 참여자 보상에 사용됩니다. 현재 자료상 ORBS는 Ethereum 기반 ERC-20 토큰이며, staking 구조는 Ethereum과 이후 Polygon까지 확장된 multi-chain 맥락으로 설명됩니다.

## Token Utility
- Settlement of fees related to app execution / infrastructure usage
- Staking
- Election of Guardians (validators)
- Rewarding PoS participants

## Staking / Incentive Layer
- Delegators stake ORBS and assign voting weight to Guardians
- Guardians secure the network and participate in consensus / operations
- Public materials describe ORBS as the incentive layer of the PoS universe

## Distribution / Rewards Notes
- Public source snippets mention historical reward allocations such as annual ORBS reward pools
- Public FAQ snippet for PoS V3 mentions a fixed APR model (needs careful version-scoped interpretation)
- Exact tokenomics should be versioned, because V1/V2/V3 mechanics may differ

## Important Notes
- Token economics should be separated into: base utility, staking incentives, market/supply snapshot, and version-specific staking mechanics
- See also `token-overview.md` for supply/market framing and `exchange-listings.md` for listing references
- Need direct tokenomics article / whitepaper extraction for more precise supply and emissions language
