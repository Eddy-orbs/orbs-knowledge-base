# Events and Campaigns

## The Orbs Agentic Challenge / Orbs Agentic Challenge

### Summary
The Orbs Agentic Challenge is a May 2026 trading campaign for Orbs Agentic / SPOT-style agent-native DeFi execution. It promotes one-trade participation, zero Orbs-side fees for the campaign flow, and USDC prizes across two independent phases.

### Event Window
- Overall campaign window: 2026-05-04 to 2026-05-24, UTC.
- Phase 1: 2026-05-04 to 2026-05-10, UTC.
- Phase 2: 2026-05-11 to 2026-05-24, UTC.

### Participation Mechanics
- Users participate by executing at least one trade through Orbs Agentic.
- The official page states that trade size can be any amount and can be on any Orbs-supported chain.
- The official X announcement frames the flow as: install the Orbs Agentic skill, execute a trade such as TWAP, limit order, or stop-loss, and enter the raffle.
- Phase 1 and Phase 2 are independent; users can join either or both.

### Prize Structure
- Total prize pool: more than 1,500 USDC.
- Phase 1 onboarding raffle:
  - One trade equals one raffle ticket.
  - Total pool: 500 USDC.
  - 10 random winners receive 50 USDC each.
  - Draw date stated on the English page: 2026-05-11.
- Phase 2 volume competition and lucky draw:
  - 1st by volume: approximately 599 USDC, described as the value of a Mac Mini M4.
  - 2nd by volume: 200 USDC.
  - 3rd by volume: 100 USDC.
  - Lucky draw: 10 random Phase 2 participants receive 50 USDC each.

### Product / Narrative Relevance
- The campaign is useful evidence that Orbs is actively positioning Orbs Agentic as a practical execution layer between AI agents and DeFi protocols.
- The page describes Orbs Agentic as supporting swaps, limit orders, TWAP, stop-loss, and take-profit through one interface, with decentralized cosigned-oracle verification before execution.
- The page describes the structure as non-custodial and gasless for the agent.
- It reinforces the SPOT / Orbs Agentic narrative: AI agents can express or construct DeFi trading intents, while Orbs provides execution verification and advanced order infrastructure.

### Rules / Caveats
- Participants must be 18+ and located where legally permitted; the promotion is void where prohibited.
- Wash trading leads to disqualification.
- Leaderboard updates may be shared on X / Twitter.
- Winners are verified before payout.
- Prizes are paid in USDC.
- Winners are contacted via on-chain address; unclaimed prizes expire 30 days after the draw.
- Orbs Network is listed as the sponsor and sole arbiter of eligibility and disqualification.
- Network gas and token costs are payable to third parties, not to Orbs.

### Sources
- English campaign page: https://www.orbs.com/challenge/
- Korean campaign page: https://www.orbs.com/ko/challenge/
- Official X announcement: https://x.com/orbs_network/status/2051583930206216517

## Bithumb ORBS Flexible Staking Events / Coin Interest Flexible Events

### Summary
Bithumb is running Korean exchange-side ORBS events under the title "코인 이자받기(스테이킹) 오브스(ORBS) 자유형 이벤트". The events reward users who agree to the flexible staking / coin-interest service and either deposit ORBS into Bithumb or buy ORBS on Bithumb during the event period. These are Bithumb custodial / exchange-side events, not direct self-custody Orbs PoS staking.

### Source Status
- Source material: user-provided image attachment interpreted on 2026-05-07.
- Public link: not yet available as of the user update; add the official public Bithumb link later once provided.

### Common Event Window
- Event period: 2026-05-08 15:00:00 to 2026-05-21 23:59:59 KST.
- Duration stated in the image: 14 days.

### Event 1: Flexible-Service Agreement + ORBS Deposit
The first image describes Event 1 as targeting users who agree to the flexible staking / coin-interest service and deposit ORBS.

Participation method:
1. Agree to and maintain use of the flexible coin-interest / staking service until reward payout.
2. Deposit ORBS into Bithumb during the event period and increase net deposit quantity.
3. Hold / maintain the ORBS quantity until the event end date and time.

### Reward Structure
Rewards are paid automatically in ORBS according to each user's ORBS net-deposit ranking during the event period.

| Rank | Per-user reward |
|---|---:|
| 1–50 | ORBS worth KRW 130,000 |
| 51–450 | ORBS worth KRW 50,000 |
| 451–1,000 | ORBS worth KRW 20,000 |

### Tie-Break Rules
If users have the same rank, higher-ranked participants are selected using the following order:
1. Higher total ORBS net-deposit quantity during the event period at the event end point.
2. Earlier first agreement date to the flexible coin-interest / staking service.

### Net Deposit Calculation
- Net deposit quantity is calculated for ORBS during the event period.
- Formula: net deposit quantity = event-period total sum of `(deposit quantity - withdrawal quantity)`.
- Deposit quantity is counted based on the point at which Bithumb completes deposit confirmation.
- Withdrawal quantity is counted based on the point at which the user requests withdrawal.

### Event 2: Flexible-Service Agreement + ORBS Purchase
The second image describes Event 2 as targeting users who agree to the flexible staking / coin-interest service and buy ORBS on Bithumb.

Participation method:
1. Agree to and maintain use of the flexible coin-interest / staking service until reward payout.
2. Buy ORBS on Bithumb during the event period and increase net purchase quantity.
3. Hold / maintain the ORBS quantity until the event end date and time.

Trading pair:
- Eligible pair: ORBS/KRW.

Reward structure:

| Rank | Per-user reward |
|---|---:|
| 1–50 | ORBS worth KRW 130,000 |
| 51–450 | ORBS worth KRW 50,000 |
| 451–1,000 | ORBS worth KRW 20,000 |

Tie-break rules:
1. Higher total ORBS net-purchase quantity during the event period at the event end point.
2. Earlier first agreement date to the flexible coin-interest / staking service.

Net purchase calculation:
- Net purchase quantity is calculated for ORBS during the event period.
- Formula: net purchase quantity = event-period total sum of `(buy quantity - sell quantity)`.
- When calculating held quantity, trading-fee quantity is not reflected.

### Reward Payout
- Benefit payout date stated in the second image: during Friday, 2026-05-29.
- Users may participate in both Event 1 and Event 2.
- Event rewards are paid in ORBS according to event results.

### Caution / Framing
- Treat this as a Korean exchange promotion and custodial staking-related event.
- Do not frame it as direct Orbs PoS staking or as a protocol-level reward program.
- Because the public link is not yet available, user-facing answers should state that the current record is based on a user-provided event image and should be updated with the official Bithumb notice link once public.
