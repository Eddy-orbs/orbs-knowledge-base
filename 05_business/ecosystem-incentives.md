# Ecosystem Incentives

## Summary
The Orbs incentive structure appears to combine ORBS-token-centered staking, delegation, and Guardian operations with product integration and broader ecosystem participation.

## Stakeholder Incentives
- Delegators: staking rewards and governance-aligned participation
- Guardians: network security, operations, rewards, reputation, and ecosystem role
- Contributors or builders: grants, ecosystem participation, and product integrations
- Partners or protocols: improved execution capabilities for their own users

## Incentive Interpretation
Orbs appears to aim for more than a simple validator-reward model. The project seems to connect product adoption with the network incentive layer, so that as protocol integrations increase, Orbs’ presence as an execution layer grows, while the staking and community layer support security and operation underneath.
