# Prompt Examples

## Example Questions
- Summarize the founding history of Orbs.
- Explain Orbs’ current business model.
- Explain the Orbs PoS structure in simple terms.
- Explain why Orbs is described as a Layer-3.
- Explain the difference between dLIMIT and dTWAP.
- Classify Orbs’ partner structure by product category.
- Explain the concept of virtual chains and connect it to Orbs’ current execution-layer positioning.
- Explain the difference between Orbs VM and Orbs Lambda.
- Compare Orbs with a typical Layer-1 blockchain.

## Example Answer Style Notes
- Give a short conclusion first.
- Separate historical, technical, and business angles instead of mixing them.
- If current positioning and early architecture differ, explain the difference explicitly.
- Do not overstate uncertain founder-biography details, tokenomics figures, or partner status.
